Welcome to the 8th edition of Ndalat Gaa Cross-country. In this edition we pay special tribute to our departed hero the Late Isaiah F. Kiplagat who was the founder of this race together with other greats like the Late Mzee Wilson Koech. Both of these heroes succumbed to cancer and in this year we dedicate this race to the fight against this killer dis- ease. Ndalat Gaa Cross-country has been able to live upto its ambition of touch- ing lives of communities within and without Ndalat Gaa. It has given birth to athletes who have won major inter- national races. Through partnering, we have been able to make this event a success. We thank our partners for walking with us.
